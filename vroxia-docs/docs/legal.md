## Regulatory Compliance Stack

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

Vroxia implements a **Tier-3 AML framework** aligned with FATF Recommendations:

| Requirement | Implementation |
|---|---|
| **Customer Due Diligence (CDD)** | Mandatory KYB for all institutional participants |
| **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)** | Applied to PEPs and high-risk jurisdictions |
| **Transaction Monitoring** | Real-time screening against OFAC, UN, EU sanctions lists |
| **Suspicious Activity Reporting** | Automated SAR generation for flagged patterns |
| **Record Keeping** | 7-year encrypted retention of transaction records |
| **Travel Rule** | VASP-to-VASP data sharing via IVMS101 standard |

### Know Your Business (KYB)

All institutional participants must complete KYB before accessing UDSX settlement:

1. **Entity verification** — Legal existence, ownership structure, beneficial owners (25%+ threshold)
2. **Director & Officer screening** — Sanctions, PEP, adverse media
3. **Business purpose review** — Alignment with FATF-permitted use cases
4. **Ongoing monitoring** — Annual re-verification + event-triggered review

### FATF Jurisdiction Coverage

Vroxia supports operations in **34 FATF-member and FATF-equivalent jurisdictions**, covering:
- All G20 nations
- FATF-style Regional Bodies (GAFILAT member states — LATAM focus)

## U.S. Regulatory Positioning

### Digital Asset Classification

UDSX is structured as a **wholesale settlement instrument**, not a security under the Howey Test:

- **No investment expectation:** UDSX is not sold to retail investors
- **No common enterprise:** Participants use UDSX for defined settlement purposes
- **No profit expectation from issuer's efforts:** UDSX value derives from its settlement utility

This analysis is consistent with the framework outlined in the **SEC's Framework for "Investment Contract" Analysis of Digital Assets (2019)** and subsequent guidance.

### Money Services Business (MSB)

Vroxia Protocol LLC is structured to comply with **FinCEN's BSA requirements** for money services businesses, including:
- Registration as an MSB with FinCEN
- Implementation of a written AML Program
- Designation of a Compliance Officer
- Independent testing of the AML Program
